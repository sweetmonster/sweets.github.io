Introduction

Dhakhan or Dhakhanh is a continent which is surrounded by the Mojanger Sea. In Dhakhan, there are roughly 125 countries, including the land of Mojang. They also have an empire called 'The Dhakhanise Empire'. They would usually have a King who would rule the whole of the continent.

King Absahad and Queen Zambia

King Absahad, the son of Ma'mad ul Suhaid, was born in 980AD. He was put in the throne as he made Dhakhan have a king again. In 1006 he met a noble woman named Zambia, and decided to marry her.
